Our story

Dallas Roofing Experts Committed to Quality

Our mission is to provide Dallas homeowners with durable, beautiful roofs through honest service and exceptional workmanship.

Founded by a team of roofing veterans, Myriad Roofing & Construction LLC was built on the belief that every home deserves a roof that's both strong and stunning. We saw too many homeowners get subpar work and empty promises, so we set out to change that. Starting with just a few tools and a commitment to doing things right, we've grown into a trusted name in Dallas roofing.

Today, we serve neighborhoods from Lakewood to Highland Park, treating every home like our own. Our team continues to invest in training, materials, and technology to deliver roofs that stand up to Texas weather. We're proud of the relationships we've built and the peace of mind we provide.
