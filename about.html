Our story

Built on Quality, Driven by Integrity

To provide Dallas homeowners with roofing solutions that combine exceptional craftsmanship, premium materials, and honest service – ensuring every roof we touch adds lasting value and peace of mind.

Myriad Roofing & Construction was founded on a simple belief: that every homeowner deserves a roof they can trust. Our founder, a Dallas native with over 15 years in the construction industry, saw too many families left with leaky roofs and empty promises. Determined to change that, he built Myriad around transparency, quality, and local pride.

Today, we’ve completed over 1,200 roofs across the Dallas metro area, earning a reputation for reliability and meticulous work. Our team of certified installers undergoes continuous training to stay ahead of the latest techniques and safety standards. We’re not just a roofing company – we’re your neighbors, committed to protecting what matters most.
