Storm Damage Restoration

Expert help with hail, wind, and storm damage – including insurance claims assistance.

Schedule this service

Overview

Dallas storms can be brutal. Our team specializes in assessing and repairing storm damage quickly. We work with your insurance company, provide detailed documentation, and fight to get you the coverage you deserve. From tarping to full restoration, we handle the entire process so you can focus on recovery.

What's included

  • Insurance claim assistance and advocacy
  • Free storm damage inspection
  • Temporary tarping and emergency protection
  • Restoration that meets code requirements

How it works

1

Damage Assessment

We inspect for hail hits, wind lift, and hidden damage, then document everything.

2

Insurance Liaison

We help file your claim, meet the adjuster, and ensure all damage is covered.

3

Restoration & Repair

Once approved, we complete the repairs or replacement to pre-storm condition.
